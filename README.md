### `$ cat about.md`

I'm an embedded software engineer specializing in **Embedded Linux on ARM Cortex** platforms. My work sits at the boundary between hardware and software — building and flashing custom Linux images with **Yocto**, writing low-level code in **C** and **Rust**, and automating everything around it with **Python** and **Bash**.

Currently in my 6th semester of Computer Science at **TU Dortmund**, and writing my Bachelor's thesis. Looking to move into a full embedded/software engineering role after graduation.

I like understanding systems down to the metal — reading real firmware and kernel code, not just using libraries.
